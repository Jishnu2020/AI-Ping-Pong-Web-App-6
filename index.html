AI PING PONG GAME









Creator : Jishnu

Instructions

Move your Right and Left Wrist To Move the paddle Up and Down.

Hit the ball with the paddle to bounce the ball to the other side of the canvas.

If the ball reaches your goal then the opponent gets a point.

If the ball reaches the opponent's goal then you get a point.

The opponent is controlled by AI and it is quite good, so Good Luck Gamer!
